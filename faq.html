Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products, shipping, returns, and more.

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

We offer multiple shipping options:

  • Standard Delivery: 5-7 business days (Free)
  • Express Delivery: 2-3 business days (₹99)
  • Next Day Delivery: Next business day (₹199)

Yes, we ship to all major cities and towns across India. Standard delivery is available for all locations, while express and next-day delivery are available for metro cities and select locations.

We accept all major payment methods including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
  • UPI (PayTM, PhonePe, Google Pay)
  • Digital Wallets (PayTM, Amazon Pay, Mobikwik)
  • Cash on Delivery (COD)

Yes, all our products are 100% genuine and sourced directly from manufacturers or authorized distributors. We guarantee authenticity and quality for all items sold on our platform.

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the courier partner's website.

If you receive a damaged product, please contact our customer service within 24 hours of delivery. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund, and you won't be charged for return shipping.

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders. Please contact our sales team at sales@homeessentials.com for custom quotes on orders above ₹10,000.

Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, if the order hasn't been shipped, you can request cancellation by contacting customer service. Once shipped, you can return the item as per our return policy.

Currently, we operate exclusively online to provide the best prices and widest selection. However, we're planning to open physical stores in major cities soon. Stay tuned for updates!
